Plot

The year is 1936: Professor of archaeology, Dr. Indiana Jones is approached by the US Government about an enormous excavation being undertaken by German Nazis in Egypt. With the help of his worldwide network of contacts and past love interests, Indy discovers that the Nazis are searching for the legendary "Ark of the Covenant" featured in the Old Testament, and is able to outwit the fascists to recover the unspeakably powerful artifact before it can be put to their sinister purposes.
